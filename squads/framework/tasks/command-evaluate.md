# Command of the Message Evaluate

## Processo de Avaliação

1. **Identificar Before Scenarios** articulados
2. **Identificar Negative Consequences** da inação
3. **Mapear Required Capabilities** mencionadas
4. **Avaliar Positive Business Outcomes** prometidos
5. **Verificar Differentiators** e **Proof Points**
6. **Calcular score**

## Dimensões

| Dimensão | Peso |
|----------|------|
| Before Scenarios | 20% |
| Negative Consequences | 20% |
| Required Capabilities | 15% |
| Positive Business Outcomes | 20% |
| Differentiators | 10% |
| Proof Points | 15% |
